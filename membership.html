What does membership fund?

Public records requests, SDCI and KCRHA accountability research, parcel intelligence, policy briefs, and direct engagement with city officials and property owners. All of it is published to members.

Is this tax deductible?

Burnham Civic is organized as a 501(c)(4) social welfare organization (formation pending). Contributions are not tax deductible as charitable donations but may be deductible as business expenses. Consult your tax advisor.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Email operations@burnhamcivic.org and your membership ends immediately. No contracts.
