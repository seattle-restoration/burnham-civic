Economic Taking Invoices

Each business departure is an economic taking. Each taking has a cost. Each cost is invoiced to the jurisdiction whose policies caused it. These are not symbolic. They are line-item accountings of tax revenue, jobs, and economic activity lost to policy failure.

Modeled on the New Jersey Act of December 20, 1781, which authorized county appraisers to inventory damages from government failure and file them with the legislature.
